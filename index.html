We have entered a new era of American history. Around the nation, communities are making their voices heard through organized marches and peaceful protest, galvanized by the power of group action. Pizza for Protesters wants to provide a new and inclusive avenue of action: Pizza.

We founded Pizza for Protesters because we believe that when it comes to political action, every effort makes a difference; that protest can be as simple as sharing a slice with a stranger; and, most of all, that revolution can be joyful...and delicious.

How does it work?

  1. Supporters from across the nation donate a couple of bucks
  2. Our team places orders for pizza delivery
  3. Local pizza shops near protest sites receive these orders and make the pizzas
  4. Pizzas arrive at the scene of the protest, received by supporters on the ground who distribute it to their peers

More About Us

What is your mission?

Our mission is to connect and support a national community of like-minded protesters -- through pizza!

Is this a partisan organization?

Nope! We support any protests that do not promote hateful speech or violent actions.

What type of pizza do you send?

Where do you get your pizza from?

We use SliceLife to find pizza places close to the lines since they let us tip and don't require someone to sign on delivery. We like to go with the local spots as much as possible.

What if there are funds left over?

Every dollar collected will go towards feeding hungry Americans. Any funds not spent will be saved for future hungry voters and/or marchers.
